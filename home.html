About Us

Your Safe Space
to Get Stronger

Ethis Fitness Center is more than a gym — it's a movement. Located at No.31/14, S, Kavarai St, Allpettai, Manjakkuppam, Cuddalore, we're designed for everyone. We combine physical training with a foundation of values, helping every member build strength, confidence, and balance in life.

"The gym is comfortable and safe for everyone. The trainer is polite, knowledgeable, and friendly. They give continuous positive encouragement and organise classes in a very structured manner." – Justdial Member Review

Our certified trainer provides personalised attention to every member — from correct posture guidance to customised workout plans — ensuring you see real results in a safe, comfortable, home-like environment.

We combine modern fitness equipment with physiotherapy expertise, helping you build strength, improve health, and regain confidence every single day.
